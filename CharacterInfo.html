Dislyte Patch (3.1.7)

Abigail

Frigga

“If you want to win, you need to sacrifice the useless pieces on the board.”

Legendary
Support
Summary

She, the Frigga’s power, is the queen of the snowfields.

Abigail is a defensive Support Esper who is also good at dealing damage. When Thorny Brambles deals damage to a single target, it has a chance of inflicting Sleep. Rose Blessing grants DEF Up and a Shield to all allies. Queen’s Gift increases 1 ally’s AP. If the ally whose AP was increased has not fallen, this ability’s cooldown will be reduced.

Pros & Cons
  • Support with good utilty
  • Has attack up, absorb (lifesteal for allies) and revive an ally on skill 3
  • Skill 2 gives defense up and shield for allies + self recovery buff
  • Universal HP leader
  • Skill 3 cooldown is long
  • Lacks damage
Abilities
Thorny Brambles Active

Attacks 1 enemy. Deals 120% ATK damage. Inflicts Sleep for 1 turn.

Heals 3% of Frigga's max HP.

Rose Blessing Active

Gains Recovery for 2 turns. Grants DEF Up and a Shield to all allies for 2 turns. Shield strength equals to 13% of Frigga's max HP + 25% of the target's lost HP.

Cooldown 4 turns

Queen's Gift Active

Increases 1 ally's AP by 100%. If the ally has fallen, revives them first and then heals 10% of their max HP. If the ally is not fallen, reduces this ability's cooldown by 3 turn(s).
Grants Absorb and ATK Up to all allies for 2 turns

Cooldown 7 turns

Absorb: Dealing damage restores HP equal to 20% of damage.

Captain Ability

Increases ally HP by 30%
